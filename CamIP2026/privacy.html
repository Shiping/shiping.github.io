CamIP2026 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-05-27

Data Collection

CamIP2026 does not collect personal data for the developer.

The app does not require an account, does not include advertising, does not include analytics, and does not upload your camera or microphone data to a developer-operated cloud service.

Camera, Microphone, and Local Network Access

CamIP2026 requests access to the camera and microphone so it can capture media from your device. It requests local network access so other devices on the same network can connect to the stream address shown in the app.

When the server is running, devices that can reach your local network may be able to view the stream or snapshot endpoints. Use CamIP2026 only on networks you trust.

Data Sharing

CamIP2026 does not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Video and snapshot data is served directly from your device to clients that connect to the local network address displayed by the app.

Children

CamIP2026 is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
