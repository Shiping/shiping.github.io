Common Setup

  1. Connect your iPhone or iPad and your viewing device to the same Wi-Fi network.
  2. Open CamIP2026.
  3. Allow camera, microphone, and local network permissions.
  4. Start the camera and server.
  5. Open the URL shown in the app from another device on the same network.

Troubleshooting

If the stream does not load, confirm both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network, local network permission is enabled for CamIP2026, and port 8080 is reachable.

If video is slow, lower the resolution or move both devices closer to the Wi-Fi access point.

If the camera does not start, open iOS Settings and confirm camera and microphone permissions are enabled for CamIP2026.

Privacy

CamIP2026 is designed for local-network streaming and does not require an account. Read the Privacy Policy.
